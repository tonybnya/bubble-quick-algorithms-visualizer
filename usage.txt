USER MANUAL:

Command:
'python3 main.py -option length'

'option' to choose for the Sorting Algorithm
b for Bubble Sort 
i for Insertion Sort 
m for Merge Sort 
q for Quick Sort 
s for Selection Sort 

'length' is the length of the array on which the algortihm will run

NB: Assume that all the numbers will be positive integers
and pick in the the range of 1-100.
